Soulmate Sonata


Release date: 10/04/2019


Making a classical record has always been a dream of mine. Regardless of how busy I became in life, I never let the dream perish. Now it's finally here for the world to hear.



Soulmate Sonata was inspired by the women in my life. These women inspired me to write the most beautiful music that I have written. I wanted to capture the essense of their souls and energy in this music.
